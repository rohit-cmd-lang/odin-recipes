Apple gujiya

Description

Gujiya is a traditional dessert recipe which is popular among people of all age groups and is amazingly delicious. Till now several variations of gujiya have been made and Apple Gujiya is one of them, which is a delight for those who relish this sweet dish.

Prepared using all-purpose flour and ghee, this amazing dessert recipe has a filling of sauteed apples, khoya, nuts and raisins. An interesting sweet dish recipe that you can make for your friends and family on festivals and special occasions. Try this amazingly delicious gujiya recipe and enjoy with your loved ones!

Ingredients

  • 4 cup all purpose flour
  • 2 cup ghee and 1/2 teaspoon baking sode
  • 1 1/2 cup apple
  • 600 gm khoya
  • 300gm powdered sugar

Steps

  1. Peel and grate apple
  2. prepare the dough for gujiya.
  3. Fry Khoya, mix all other ingredients and set aside
  4. saute apple and fix it with filling
  5. Prepare gujiya and add filling
  6. Deep fry and serve hot
